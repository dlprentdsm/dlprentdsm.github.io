Consulting.

Data Science is my passion. Vetic Analytics harnesses the power of big data to deliver big insights with real business value.

Why choose Vetic Analytics?

I have expertise in cutting edge statistics and machine learning.

I'm currently a Data Scientist at a large tech company, where I work independently to find value in big data, both by delivering insights to key process owners and by building and deploying automated models. In the past I've worked as a consultant for veterinary swine flu research, and helped build semantic search capabilities at Microsoft Research Asia. I'm also PhD student in Statistics at the University of Minnesota, which requires advanced mathematics and strong computer programming and technical skills. I know how to work with little data and big data, experimental and observational data, clean data and messy data, all using the principled framework of statistics. Statistics is truly the science of data, so a just like a plumber is the expert in plumbing, or a car mechanic is the expert on cars, a statistician is the expert in interpreting and analyizing data.

Along with my expertise in data science, I also bring strong interpersonal and communication skills to the table. From our first meetings and listening to you to understand the important business logic that will underly my analysis, to communicating my final results in a clear and concise way that will help you make a confident decision, to presenting my findings to other key stakeholders so that you can get their buy-in, you can count on me to distill complex technical ideas into plain language.

Most importantly though, I am highly client focused. I pay attention to YOU, and I want to help YOU. My goal is your success.
