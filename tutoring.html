Tutoring.

I am passionate about teaching. If you are struggling in math, statistics, economics or computer science, I can help.

Why choose me?

I have been tutoring in Mathematics and Statistics for the past 8 years and have a passion for helping others learn.

I've tutored since my time at Grinnell College and I now offer my services to both University of Minnesota students and the wider community. I'm currently a PhD student in Statistics at the University of Minnesota, so I have great expertise in this area. A PhD program in statistics also requires advanced mathematics and strong computer programming and technical skills, so I am highly qualified to assist with these subjects too. Most importantly though, I also bring strong interpersonal skills to the table. I pay attention to YOU, and I want to help YOU. You are not just one of thirty students in a classroom- you are YOU, and you have a unique way of learning that I respect. My goal is your success.

Subjects I can help with:

  • Statistics
  • Math
  • Physics
  • Economics
  • Computer Science
  • General computing assistance

Rates and Availability

I charge a flat hourly rate of $50 per hour for the first session, and $40 per hour after that. I am quite flexible and I am sure I can work you into my schedule. Please contact me to schedule a session.

Affordability - Split the Cost with a Friend

As a PhD student I am very busy, and my time is valuable. My rate reflects this. One way to mitigate the cost of tutoring is to split the cost with a classmate. Many tutors discourage this, but I simply charge a flat rate for my time. Therefore, by splitting a tutoring session with someone else you can dramatically reduce the cost. For example:

My Flat Rate             Cost to you
You: $40 per hour $40 per hour
You and a friend: $40 per hour $20 per hour
You and your study group: $40 per hour $8 per hour (with 5 people)
